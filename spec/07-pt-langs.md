## Portuguese

### Unicode Support

All apps aimed to support Portuguese MUST support the following Unicode blocks:

1. Basic Latin (U+0000..U+007F).
2. Latin-1 Supplement (U+00A0..U+00FF). This range excludes the need for supporting C1 Controls.
